Create Your Wallet

You have to sync with the blockchain to use BTScoin. BTS is a "light" wallet. It syncs with the blockchain by "skimming" through the blockchain, providing fast sync times. BTS Core, on the other hand, is a "full" wallet. It syncs by downloading it, providing a solid-working BTScoin wallet

Start Sending BTScoin

BTScoin wallets are applications that let you interact with your BTScoin account. Think of it like an internet banking app – without the bank. Your wallet lets you read your balance, send transactions and connect to applications.
